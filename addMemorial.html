How to Add a Memorial

To add a memorial to the Rider Memorials Map, please follow these steps:

  1. Drop a pin on either Google or Apple Maps where the memorial is located.
  2. Copy the coordinates from that pin. The coordinates should look something like this: 46.2115, -119.1225
  3. Email these coordinates to kimmerandcasper@gmail.com, including the following information:
    • The full name of the fallen rider (with correct spelling).
    • A picture of the fallen rider.
    • A brief description of how the rider died.
    • The year of birth and death of the rider (e.g., 1985-2023).
    • Any additional information you'd like to include about the rider.
